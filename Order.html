Booking information

Here you can book a petsitter! We do not ask for you card information yet. You will enter your dates, type of care, what animals need care, and then provide us with your information. The petsitter assigned to you will then contact you and make sure we have all the details right. We'll send you an electronic contract, and we'll be all set! Please note this contract is still legally binding.

Please make sure that you've read our services page, and checked the help page for anything you're unsure about! If you're still unsure, contact us! We'll be happy to help you with any concerns.
