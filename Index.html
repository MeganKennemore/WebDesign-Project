About our company

Megan's PetSitters takes care of your pets as if they were our own. You don't have to worry when you go away, because we'll be there to give your pet all the love and comfort they'll be missing. You don't have to pay expensive boarding fees or stress your pets by removing them from their familiar space - our petsitters will come to your home! Our petsitters can for your pets in two ways. If you would like round the clock care, our petsitters will stay in your house with your pets and care for them. If you would just like someone to check in on your beloved pets, our petsitters will come in daily and spend a few hours caring for and playing with your pets.

We will care for dogs, cats, birds, rodents, fish, and non carnivourous reptiles and amphibians. Unfortunately our staff does not have the training to safely handle and feed carnivourous reptiles and amphibians. For more detailed information about what pets we can responsibly care for, vist our Services page.

About our employees

Megan's pets has five petsitters in addition to our founder Megan: Sequoia, Aaron, Shree, Jackson, and Jon. While some of them are more experienced with some animals than others, they are all capable of caring for all pets we offer services for. We throughly check the background of all our petsitters, and all are free of any criminal records. Aaron, Sequoia, and Jackson are veterinary students, and our other employees are students as well. They are all capable of administering medicine and other care to your special needs pet. After booking with us, your animal's petsitter will call and introduce themselves, as well as send a picture, so that you know who you're leaving your furry friends with.
